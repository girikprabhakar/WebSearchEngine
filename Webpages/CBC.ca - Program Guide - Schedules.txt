Hosted by Jason D'Souza. 9:00 AM The Sunday Magazine The Sunday Magazine is a lively, wide-ranging mix of long-form conversations, engaging ideas and music. 11:00 AM What On Earth Laura Lynch grapples with the challenges of climate change and explores potential solutions. 12:00 PM Cost of Living An entertaining & revealing look at the way almost every decision we make ties us to the larger economy. 12:30 PM The Doc Project Introducing the next generation of storytellers 1:00 PM Spark On-air and online, join Nora Young for a surprising and irreverent look at tech, trends, and fresh ideas. 2:00 PM Tapestry Tapestry is your guide through the messy business of being human, your time to pause and go deep. 3:00 PM Writers & Company Explore the thoughts and works of remarkable writers from around the world. 4:00 PM Cross Country Checkup Tune in each week for a lively discussion on an issue of national interest, hosted by Ian Hanomansing 6:00 PM The World This Weekend The World This Weekend is a comprehensive half-hour news and information program. 6:30 PM White Coat, Black Art White Coat, Black Art returns with more stories from behind the scenes of health and health care. 7:00 PM Radiolab Radiolab is where sound illuminates ideas, & boundaries blur between science, philosophy & human experience. 8:00 PM This American Life The stories presented in This American Life are engaging, intimate, surprising, funny, disturbing, and bittersweet. 9:00 PM PlayME Transforming the way we experience theatre by turning contemporary plays into bingeable audio dramas. 10:00 PM Snap Judgment Snap Judgment mixes real stories with killer beats to produce cinematic, dramatic radio. 11:00 PM WireTap Tune in and eavesdrop as Jonathan Goldstein talks over the phone with some of the country's best storytellers. 11:30 PM Short Cuts Audio documentary shorts from around the world.
