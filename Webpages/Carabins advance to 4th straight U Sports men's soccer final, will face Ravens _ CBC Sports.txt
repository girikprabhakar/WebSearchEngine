LiveWatch U Sports men's soccer national championship LiveWatch U Sports women's soccer national championship The Carabins took the title in 2018 when Montreal edged Cape Breton 2-1. Montreal lost the 2017 final 3-2 to Cape Breton and fell 2-0 to the University of Quebec-Trois-Rivieres in the 2019 final. The 2020 championship tournament was cancelled because of COVID-19. Women's championship The MacEwan Griffins and Trinity Western Spartans clash for the U Sports women's soccer crown Sunday in Sydney, N.S. The Griffins advanced with a 1-0 win in extra time Saturday over the UBC Thunderbirds. Fifth-year forward Meagan Lemoine scored in the 96th minute for MacEwan.
