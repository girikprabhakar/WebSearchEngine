Story by Saara Chaudry and CBC Kids News • 2021-09-10 06:00 Hint: They might be trying to sell you something What influences an influencer? Money. For many people, it’s a career, after all. Some influencers make a living promoting brands, selling their own merchandise or subscriptions and accepting fan donations. But the big money comes from ads. In theory, influencers are supposed to be really upfront about it when they’re being paid to promote. Of course, that doesn’t always happen, so you have to pay attention.
